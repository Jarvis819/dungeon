📖 HOW TO PLAY

🎯 Goal

Reach Level 19 (13 HP) and click the Dark Lord at row 6, col 7 (center-ish) to win!

⛏ Starting

Click the ⛏ Pickaxe tile to begin — reveals 12 tiles in a cross/diamond. A hidden 2nd pickaxe exists with the same effect.

⚔ Combat & clues

Click to reveal tiles. If monster power > your HP → instant death. Revealed empty tiles show remaining adjacent threat (sum of enemy P); it updates when neighbors are cleared. You briefly see who you’re fighting before damage resolves.

🎯 Threat marks (notes)

Right-click to set P1–P11 or Mine (P100). Marks are your notes only — clue numbers always show the full adjacent threat sum. When your marks around a clue account for that sum (same total power), click the clue (or middle-click) to chord. Press F for mark mode. Digging / chord / pickaxe clears a mark on reveal.

⭐ EXP & Manual Level Up

Kill Px = x EXP. When you have enough EXP, a ✨ LEVEL UP button appears. Click it to spend EXP and go up one level (+1 HP cap + full heal). EXP stacks — you won't auto-level.

❤️ 0 HP Survival & Hearts

At 0 HP you can still dig and guess — revealing a monster you can't survive (power > your HP) ends the run. Mines still explode. Collect a ❤️ on a tile or level up to recover.

🧱 Bricks (x6)

Click 1: Revealed (no damage). Click 2,3,4: 1 damage each. After 4 clicks: destroyed, gives +1 EXP.

👹 Specials

🧛 P9 Vampire: drops ❤️ on same tile after kill (click to collect later).
🐉 P10 Dragon: always at a board corner — Mine Slayer (mines → +3 EXP).
📦 P11 Mimic: hides in one chest — neighbor clues count it as P11 (safe chests count 0).
👁️ P5 Eye (P5): 12 tiles around it are “mystery” (look like normal stone — doesn’t reveal the Eye). They block auto-clear until the Eye is killed.
🃏 P0 Spider Card: marks every P1 spider tile with 🕷️ + P1 (not a generic flag).
🐵 P0 Monkey Card: +9 EXP free.
💣 Mine P100: instant death (or +3 EXP with Mine Slayer).

🗺️ After defeat & debug

When you lose, the board shows the full spawn layout; the defeat panel sits at the bottom with a light dim so you can still see the map. Press ` (backtick) during a run to toggle debug peek (testing).
